## Where to find free Indian stock photos

- **Unsplash**: search "India family home", "India business", "Delhi building", "Indian office"
- **Pexels**: similar searches — all free for commercial use
- **Pixabay**: good for Indian lifestyle/business images
- **Freepik**: requires attribution unless on paid plan

## Important

- Save files as **JPEG** (`.jpg`) for best performance
- Keep file size under **300 KB** per image (use https://squoosh.app to compress)
- Minimum width: **900px** for service/banner images, **600px** for partner cards
- Avoid images with embedded text or watermarks
